North Valley Philatelic Exhibition (NOVAPEX)

Each year the Redding Stamp club hosts the North Valley Philatelic Exhibition during the first weekend in March. The show draws a minimum of 8 stamp dealers from California and Oregon and includes the United States Post Office.

Dealers intersted in attending the show should contact Allen Fracchia at 530.227.2133. (email: ajrv1938@charter.net)

Exhibitors intersted in showcasing their exhibits should contact Mark Woodward at 530.221.8933. (email: markwoodward@charter.net)

Copies of the official entry form and instructions are available on the downloads page or at the top of this page.
